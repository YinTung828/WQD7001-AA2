The data science problem faced in tourism industry is the poor tourism experience. An excellent tourism experience is in essence since the tourism industry play a role in contributing to global economy by creating multiples of jobs. Tourism can be vital for travelers. Travelers can improve their understanding of other people cultures. If the experience of tourism is poor, the tourism industry will be affected heavily. Hence, it is important to provide an excellent experience that can cater the interest of tourists. In order to improve the tourism experience, the data science is employed to analyze historical travel data and preference of travelers. With the utilization of data science, travelers can receive suggestions for destination where they prefer.
